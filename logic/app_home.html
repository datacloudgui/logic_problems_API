This app solve two logical problems.

1. Ping Pong Game.

Ana, Jose and Juan play ping pong, each of them play the quantity of games viewed below.

If a player lose the game, in the next game the waiting player plays against the winner.

Player------------Games played

Player1: Ana-----17

Player2: Jose----15

Player3: Juan---10

Question: Who is the loser of the second game? and which games this person lose?

The next link provide the answer with the games played showed above.

Ping pong solution

If you want simulate the "Find second loser" with other scores add the follow parameters to the URL.

/api/v1/games/secondloser?player1=12&player2=10&player3=5

By default players are in the same order showed previously. Ex: Player1: Ana

Example with different parameters

Notice that if its not posible calculate the second loser a message show the why.
